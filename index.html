Election Commission

The legal right to exercise your voting powers to choose the people who govern and run the place is perhaps one of the greatest human achievements of all time. This gift should be protected, preserved and passed on with great care. And the most powerful way to make sure that this proud legacy is carried on is to be a part of it. One vote may seem miniscule in some sense, but going out there to vote and vote for change is perhaps the greatest contribution one can make to make sure the society is free. Do vote because every vote counts.
